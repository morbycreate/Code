Our Story

BBQ Test was born from a passion for authentic barbecue and a mission to share championship-level products with grill masters everywhere. Founded by pit masters who refused to compromise on quality, we hand-select every product in our collection.

From competition-grade rubs crafted by award-winning pitmasters to award-winning sauces that have won hearts at cookouts nationwide, every item reflects our commitment to excellence. We believe that great BBQ starts with great products.

Today, BBQ Test serves over 50,000 grill masters who trust us to deliver the gear, sauces, and seasonings that make legendary cookouts possible.
